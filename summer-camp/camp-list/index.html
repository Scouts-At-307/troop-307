Scouts are to be at the Scout Hut by 8AM Sunday morning in Class A uniforms ready to go. Bring a bag lunch for Sunday. Pack a swimsuit, swim shoes, towel on top of gear as the swim test is the first activity after the Troop orientation. We will be returning Saturday by 1:30PM. It’s a good idea to label all items/clothing/bedding. Scouters do laundry for the Troop Wednesday evening and would like each boy to contribute $1 for costs. This is requested, not mandatory. Scouts have several opportunities to earn money for Camp and are encouraged to take advantage of these. If any Scout still needs financial assistance after taking advantage of these opportunities, please speak with Mike Gallagher, Robert Sears or Richard Brock.

Visitors Night

Parents and visitors are permitted to eat in the dining hall on WEDNESDAY NIGHT ONLY with their Scout and his unit BY PRIOR TICKET PURCHASE AT THE CAMP DIRECTORS OFFICE by no later than Wednesday. WEDNESDAY is Visitors Night. Parents are encouraged to send a Care Package to their Scout and NOT attend Visitors night. Many Scouts, especially first year Scouts, tend to become homesick and end up going home with their parents so the Troop does not encourage visitation. This is a growth opportunity for your Scout.

Medication

ALL medications will be turned in to and administered by Troop 307 Leaders and/or Camp Rainey Mountain Medical Staff.
